UPCOMING WEBINAR

Still saying no to AI tools because of security risks? There’s a better way.

Wednesday, April 8th
11:00 AM PST

AI tools like Cursor, n8n, and Lovable are rapidly transforming how teams build, automate, and innovate - yet many organizations still default to blocking them due to security concerns.

In this webinar, we’ll explore the modern CISO’s dilemma: how to enable AI-driven productivity without losing control.

We’ll discuss the new risks introduced by AI builders, why traditional security approaches fall short, and what a practical, enforceable framework for AI workspace security looks like.

Join us to learn how security teams can move from “no” to a confident, controlled “yes.”
