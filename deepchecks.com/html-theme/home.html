Evaluation is Complex

LLM applications have no test set, causing a measurement problem. Without standardized
performance metrics, credible auto-scoring, and streamlined version comparisons, every evaluation
task becomes "a project"- slowing down innovation and increasing risk.

A small change in the answer might change the meaning of the answer completely.

Organizations face growing challenges in evaluating LLM performance
consistently, scaling their LLM apps to production, and making reliable, data-
driven decisions. Deepchecks' platform streamlines a process of configuring
auto-scoring and applying it to your LLM-based app during dev, CI/CD and
production.

Why Choose Deepchecks

Generative AI produces subjective results. Knowing whether a generated text is good usually requires
manual labor by a subject matter expert.

Superior Accuracy
